Brief Summary

This website is about 2000s Garage rock revival bands.It's a genre of indie rock that developed in the late 1990s and early 2000s, inspired by the original sounds and aesthetics of garage rock of the 1960s and new wave and post-punk of the 1980s. There are bands that are not mentioned in this web,like The Killers, Franz Ferdinad, Bloc Party. Comment at the front page if you want to hear other music.
